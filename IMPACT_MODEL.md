# ET AI Concierge (Track 7) - Impact Model (Back-of-the-envelope)

## What “impact” means here
Because the prototype is a concierge experience (not an execution engine), the measurable value is primarily about:
1. Faster discovery of the “right thing” within ET ecosystem.
2. Improved re-engagement for lapsed users.
3. Reduced friction at life-event moments (e.g., home loan intent).

## Metric targets (assumptions you can cite)
Assume an average ET user session where the user currently:
- spends ~8-12 minutes clicking around before finding relevant content
- has a meaningful chance to bounce without finding a next step (approx. 10-20%)

### 1) Reduced time-to-value (TTV)
- Baseline: 10 minutes to locate helpful guidance (avg).
- Concierge: structured next steps reduce exploration time by ~30-50%.
- Estimated time saved per engaged user: 3-5 minutes.

Example math:
- If 50,000 users/week try the concierge flow and 40% would have “continued searching”,
  weekly minutes saved ≈ 50,000 * 0.40 * 4 = 80,000 minutes (~1,333 hours).

### 2) Higher content/product discovery rate
- Baseline “helpful click” rate (assume 12%).
- Concierge improves it by +20-40% relative due to persona-aware ranking (assume +3-5 pp absolute).
- Estimated incremental engaged users:
  - 50,000/week * (3-5% of all users) = 1,500–2,500 additional engaged users/week.

### 3) Re-engagement uplift (lapsed ET Prime)
- Baseline return rate after 90 days lapse (assume 5%).
- Scenario-aligned re-engagement + “new value quickly” could lift return by +1.5–2.5 pp.
- Estimated incremental returning users:
  - 20,000 lapsed users/week * 0.015–0.025 = 300–500 additional returning users/week.

### 4) Cross-sell conversion lift (home loan intent)
- Life-event moments create intent. Baseline conversion at click-through might be ~0.5–1.0%.
- Concierge reduces mismatch via structured recommendations; assume a +20-30% relative uplift.
- Example incremental conversions:
  - If 30,000 users/week show home-loan intent signals,
    baseline conversions ≈ 30,000 * 0.7% = 210
    uplift 25% => +52–+53 extra conversions/week.

## Why the numbers are defendable
- The prototype explicitly targets the scenario pack’s “next action” requirement, which is what reduces exploration time.
- The audit trail and deterministic mapping make the prototype’s logic transparent, so judges can accept the mechanism behind the uplift.

## Suggested pitch line (use in 3-minute video)
"We’re not adding another chatbot. We compress the user journey into a multi-step onboarding flow with persona-aware product mapping and an audit trail. The expected outcome is faster time-to-value and measurable uplift in product discovery and re-engagement."
